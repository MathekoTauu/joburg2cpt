About the Run

Tshediso Mahange is embarking on a remarkable 1400 km run from Johannesburg to Cape Town. His mission is to promote educational opportunities and inspire communities across South Africa.

Follow Tshediso as he runs from October 1, 2023 to November 15, 2023, with key milestones along the way.

This incredible journey is more than just a run - it's a movement to bring educational resources to underserved communities and inspire the next generation of South African leaders.

Training & Preparation

Behind the scenes of Tshediso's incredible journey

Tshediso has been preparing for this challenge for over a year, with a rigorous training regimen designed to build both physical endurance and mental resilience.

His training includes:

  • Long-distance running (40-60 km per day)
  • Strength and conditioning workouts
  • Nutrition planning with sports dietitians
  • Mental preparation and visualization techniques

"This journey is as much about mental strength as physical endurance. I'm running to show South African youth that with determination and preparation, any goal is achievable."
