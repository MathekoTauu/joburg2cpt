The Journey

More than just a run - it's a movement for educational change in South Africa

Tshediso Mahange is embarking on an extraordinary 1400km journey from Johannesburg to Cape Town, running to promote educational opportunities and inspire communities across South Africa.

This incredible mission spans October 2025 to November 2025, covering key cities and communities along the route, bringing hope and resources to underserved schools.

Every kilometer represents a step toward educational equality, every donation makes a lasting impact, and every supporter becomes part of a movement that will shape South Africa's future.

Behind the Mission

The dedication, preparation, and vision driving this extraordinary journey

Tshediso has dedicated over 14 months to preparing for this monumental challenge, combining rigorous physical training with strategic planning to maximize the mission's impact.

Training Regiment:

  • Daily long-distance runs (40-60km)
  • Strength conditioning and injury prevention
  • Sports nutrition optimization
  • Mental resilience and visualization training
  • Route planning and community engagement
"This journey represents more than physical endurance - it's about showing South African youth that with determination, preparation, and community support, any dream is achievable." - Tshediso Mahange
